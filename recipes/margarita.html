Soup's Recipe Book: Margarita on the Rocks

Description

Okay fuckers, this is actually a real recipe. This margarita will knock your socks off.

Ingredients

  • Jose Cuervo Gold tequila
  • Gran Marnier orange liquer
  • Fresh lime or lemon juice
  • Minute Maid Limeade Concentrate
  • Rock salt or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes. Seriously don't skip this part.

Recipe

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine 1 part Grand Marnier, 3 scant parts Cuervo, scant 1 part limeade concentrate (Minute Maid is by far the best) and 1/2 part fresh lemon or lime juice.
  2. Shake thoroughly
  3. (Optional) Rim glass with rock salt or sugar using a little bit of juice or marg.
  4. Fill glass all the way with ice cubes
  5. I said FILL. None of this 3 cubes shit. This drink is like 29% abv, the ice helps.
  6. Pour margarita into glass
  7. Enjoy responsibly. Or irresponsibly. I'm not your fucking mom.
